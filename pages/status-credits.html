💡 Multi-Leg Advantage — Reroutes & Status Runs
When you book via a connecting city, each segment earns status credits separately. A SYD→MEL direct earns 20 SC. The same journey via BNE earns 40 SC — for the same price. The "Status Run" category shows full day-return trips optimised purely for SC accumulation.
